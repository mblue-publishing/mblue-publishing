M-Blue Publishing ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information, if any, we collect when you visit mblue-publishing.com.

## Data Controller

M-Blue Publishing  
Email: [mblue.publishing@proton.me](mailto:mblue.publishing@proton.me)

## What Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

This website does not use cookies, analytics tools, tracking scripts, or any form of user profiling. We do not operate contact forms, newsletters, or user accounts. No personal information is stored, processed, or shared by us.

## Third-Party Services

This website loads fonts from **Google Fonts** (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com). When your browser requests a font file, Google's servers may log your IP address as part of standard server logging. This processing is outside our control. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details on how Google handles this data.

No other third-party services, trackers, or analytics tools are used on this website.

## Cookies

This website does not use cookies of any kind.

## Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Although we do not hold your personal data, you have rights under UK data protection law, including:

- The right to access personal data held about you
- The right to have inaccurate data corrected
- The right to have your data erased
- The right to object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, or for any privacy-related queries, please contact us at [mblue.publishing@proton.me](mailto:mblue.publishing@proton.me).

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk) if you believe your data protection rights have been breached.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
