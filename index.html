About M-Blue Publishing

Life does not always give notice before it changes. M-Blue Publishing exists for people navigating that kind of turning point, and we publish non-fiction that is honest about the difficulty and practical about what comes next.

We believe the people closest to a struggle are best placed to write about it, so we built M-Blue to support them in doing it themselves. Authors shape the book; we help it find its readers, giving independent voices the reach usually reserved for traditional publishing.
